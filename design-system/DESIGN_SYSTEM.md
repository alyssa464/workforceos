# WorkforceOS Design System

## Visual Identity
**Core Concept:** Minimalist, Executive, High-Trust, Systemic.
The brand should feel like a high-end operating system for businesses. Not a "productivity tool," but a "source of truth."

## Color Palette

### Neutrals (The Foundation)
- **Black:** `#000000` (Primary backgrounds, heavy headings)
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` (Secondary backgrounds, primary text on dark)
- **Charcoal 900:** `#111111` (Deep dark cards)
- **Charcoal 800:** `#1A1A1A` (Dark borders, secondary surfaces)
- **Charcoal 700:** `#2A2A2A` (Tertiary surfaces)
- **Gray 400:** `#9CA3AF` (Secondary text)
- **Gray 100:** `#F3F4F6` (Primary text on white backgrounds)

### Accents (The Intelligence)
- **Workforce Blue:** `#3B82F6` (Primary action, progress)
- **Cyan Soft:** `#60A5FA` (Support accent)

### Semantic Colors
- **Success:** `#10B981` (Healthy status, high independence)
- **Warning:** `#F59E0B` (Caution, moderate dependency)
- **Critical:** `#EF4444` (Danger, high bottleneck risk)

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Inter (Sans-serif)
- **Headings:** Bold/Semibold, tight tracking (-0.02em)
- **Body:** Regular, comfortable leading (1.5)

## Components Taxonomy

### 1. Cards
- Subtle borders (1px) in `Charcoal 800` or `Gray 200`
- Border radius: `8px` or `12px`
- No heavy shadows; use thin borders for depth.

### 2. Buttons
- **Primary:** Black background, white text (on light) or White background, black text (on dark).
- **Outline:** Transparent background, 1px border.
- **Ghost:** No background, subtle hover state.

### 3. Inputs
- Minimalist line or subtle box.
- Focus state: `Workforce Blue` glow or border.

### 4. Score Indicators
- Large numbers for primary scores (0-100).
- Progress rings or linear bars for secondary metrics.
- Color mapping: 0-40 (Critical), 41-70 (Warning), 71-100 (Success).

### 5. Progress Bars
- Background: `Charcoal 800`
- Fill: `Workforce Blue` or semantic color.

## Logo / Wordmark
- **Wordmark:** Inter Bold, all caps or sentence case with tight tracking. 
- **Symbol:** A stylized "W" or an abstract representation of a network/intelligence graph.
